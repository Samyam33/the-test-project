About

My name is Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro and I was born on February 5, 1985, in Madeira, Portugal. I am a professional football player and have played for some of the best clubs in the world, including Sporting CP, Manchester United, Real Madrid and Juventus.

I have won numerous individual awards throughout my career, including five Ballon d'Or awards, which is given to the best football player in the world. I am also the all-time top scorer for both Real Madrid and the Portugal national team.

Off the pitch, I am a proud father of four children and am involved in various philanthropic activities through my charity, the CR7 Foundation.
